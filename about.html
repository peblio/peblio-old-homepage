Our Mission

We want to empower teachers to teach CS in a way that promotes iteractive processes, design thinking, and creative problem solving. We're providing a framework for project based learning that makes it easy for teachers to create curricula and manage student work so that teachers can feel confident in their classrooms, and students can feel proud of their work.
